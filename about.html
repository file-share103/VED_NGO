About VED NGO

VED NGO is a non-profit organization committed to creating sustainable social change across India. Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between resources and those in need, we have been working tirelessly for over two decades to empower communities and transform lives.

Our organization operates on the principles of transparency, accountability, and community participation. We believe that sustainable change can only be achieved when communities are actively involved in the development process. Through our comprehensive programs in education, healthcare, women empowerment, livelihood, and disaster response, we have touched the lives of millions across 27+ states in India.

What sets us apart is our deep-rooted connection with local communities. We don't just implement programs; we build partnerships. Our team works closely with village leaders, local organizations, and community members to understand their unique needs and co-create solutions that are both effective and sustainable.

Over the years, we have successfully implemented 400+ projects, reaching over 2000 villages and impacting more than 20 lakh children and families annually. Our work spans from remote tribal areas to urban slums, ensuring that no one is left behind in the journey towards development.

We are grateful for the trust placed in us by our donors, volunteers, and partner organizations. Together, we continue to work towards our mission of building an inclusive society where every individual has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for growth.
